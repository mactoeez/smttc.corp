About Us

Serving quality & trust for over 55 years, Shri Mahesh Timber Trading Company is a trusted name in the timber and plywood industry. We specialize in high-quality wood and plywood products tailored to industrial, commercial, and residential needs. Our commitment to timely delivery, precision, and ethical business practices has made us one of the most trusted suppliers in the region.
